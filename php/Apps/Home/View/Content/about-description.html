KeyWa Introduction

Company Profile

Keywa devotes to become the global leader in chemical industry resource integration. Keywa creates B2P, a commercial Bussiness Model (business to platform) aiming to integrate chemical industry resources globally. Experienced in Internet and chemical industry, Keywa is committed to build a professional industry Internet model, setting up the seamless connection platform, linking to global chemical industry resources in order to lead the global chemical industry resource sharing. The rapid progress of Keywa with unique strategy perspective will lead chemical industry to a new prospect.


In response to the national strategy of “Supply-side Structural Reform” and strategy of “Internet+”, relying on the advanced chemical spot electronic trading platform, Keywa is committed to provide accurate, safe, efficient one-stop total solutions for operation and development global chemical enterprises; Keywa intends to save cost and raise efficiency for clients by optimizing structure and building a unique industry value chain. Keywa has aspired to become the most powerful chemical industry Internet enterprise worldwide, with strong equilibrium abilities and abundant resources for industry development.
